It was 1983 AD Earth Bound Time, when it all started!

It was a diffrent time back then, there were no starships, no inter glactic travel and no way to be a baller. Computers changed everything.

Personal computers were fascinating, even when they came out. Good old clunky mouce clicks could do more math than I could do in my entire life.

It was not until about the early 90's when I discovered the Internet. Now, with one click you could connect to ANYONE.

..... LIGHT BULB .....

More than a decade later, I began working with those machines; I discovered the art of programming. I used technologies like JavaScript, React, Redux, Node, MongoDB, Express, and GraphQL to name a few, building products on the internet that could tell stories, my stories.

I hope my skills, experience, and vision will contribute in the hopes of changing the world .....
